Careers in Web Design and Development Essay

Activity Instructions

Step 01

Review the following 3 websites concerning careers in web design and development.

  • Payscale: Web Developer
  • PayScale: Graphic Designer Web
  • PayScale: Web Designer

Step 02

Watch this week's infomercial videos.  Then review the 3 websites above.  Pick one that looks the most interesting to you and take a deeper look. Pay special attention to things like recommended skills, job description, and career path possibilities. There is also a list of related jobs at the bottom that are usually very interesting.

Reflect back on what you have learned in this class (assignments, videos, khan academy, readings, etc.) Prepare a one Full page or more essay with title page to the following:

  • "What did you learn from the websites on careers in web design and development?
  • What aspect of web design and development career areas do you lean toward?  Is there one that you feel fits you best and why?
  • What did you learn from this class in general?"

The essay must be typed, double spaced, a separate title page with your title and your name/class info, and a body of content that is minimum of one full page or more.  Use full sentences and good paragraphs.  Make sure that you check your essay for spelling and grammar errors.
