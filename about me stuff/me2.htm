Howdy ya’ll….Let me introduce myself:

I am an online instructor with Brigham Young University, Idaho.  I love my work and I love my students.  Computer Technology is my work, and also my hobby.

I taught in the California Public School system in what is called “The High Desert” for over 30 years in grades K through 8.  A little blurb on the way between Los Angeles and Las Vegas.

I have raised six children, one son and five daughters, thirty grandchildren and to date, five great grandchildren. 

I am married to my husband and eternal companion Leo.  We moved to El Paso, Texas in 2014 to help my youngest daughter (a 100% disabled US Army Veteran) with her family.

 

In my lifetime I have ridden my Ninja, kicked and punched my way to a third-degree black belt in TaeKwonDo, gotten Associates, Bachelors, and Masters degrees.  I sew, crochet, knit, bake and cake decorate.  I love life, and the Lord, and the Celestial Room is my favorite spot on earth.
