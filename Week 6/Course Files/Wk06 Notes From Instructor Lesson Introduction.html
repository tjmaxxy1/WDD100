Week Six

This week will:

  • Participate on the week 6 Discussion Board
  • Watch the week 6 infomercials
  • You will read or listen to Elder M. Russell Ballard's: “Sharing the Gospel Using the Internet” and then write a one full page short essay with title page about your responsibility using the internet in these times.
  • You will focus on creating your subpages for your website. Remember that your final project must include two subpages and is due by the end of week 7.  You will submit your sub page one this week (just the home page url is required since your links should work to your other pages)
  • You will be given the instructions for the final report to give you a little more time to do the work .... it will not be submitted this week.   (this final report is part of the final project that will be submitted in week 7.  There are 3 parts that will be submitted: the final report plus your site plan with the 3 wireframes added at the end (the site plan and the final report are two separate documents... do not combine them) and your site share collaborate url.
  • Finishing the week is the self assessment.
