LDS Online Responsibilities Essay

Activity Instructions

Estimated Time: 30 minutes

Step 01

Read or listen to Elder M. Russell Ballard's: “Sharing the Gospel Using the Internet” .   

The document is available here:

Sharing the Gospel

Step 02

After reading Elder Ballard's talk, write a one page (a title page and one full page or more) essay that answers the questions:

"What are our responsibilities as Latter-day Saints in regards to sharing the gospel through the internet?"

Watch the following step by step Sister A Video for this assignment:

The essay must be typed, double spaced, a separate title page with your title and your name/class info, and a body of content that is minimum of one full page or more.  Make sure that your essay has full sentences and good paragraphs and check your essay for spelling and grammar errors.  You will find a word template with a title page already attached... please use this template for your essay: 

(you must download and save the template to your computer to use the template)

  LDS Essay Template

Step 03

You must include your final word count in parentheses at the end of your essay (on the last page at the bottom).

Step 04

Go to the next page to access "Wk06 LDS Online Responsibilities Essay" and submit your essay.

Grading

This essay will be graded using the following rubric (10pts):

Using the essay template, your essay must show the following:

  • must be typed, double spaced
  • have a separate title page at the start with your title and your name/class info
  • have a body of content that is minimum of one full page or more. 
  • Make sure that you check your essay for spelling and grammar errors.

Please submit as a Word document.
